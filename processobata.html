Artist Catalogue


A collection of essays and images from late artist, Chiura Obata, laid out in a 57 page catalogue.

Download PDF

The start of this piece, like most of my pieces, began with sketching and content gathering. Because I felt like I needed to know about the artist before I designed a catalogue dedicated to him, my content gathering process included a lot of research and note taking. After the bulk of my research was done and content gathered, I went through several more rounds of sketching, planning title pages, the table of contents, image heavy pages, etc.

Only after these rounds of sketching did I begin work on the computer. I still tend to make a lot of changes to the larger design scheme once I move to digital, so there was a lot of changes made throughout this process.
